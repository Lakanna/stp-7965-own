FAQ

How do I start playing?

Download the game from Google Play, complete the tutorial, and start your journey through the stars.

Are there in-app purchases?

Yes, you can purchase exclusive ships, advanced upgrades, and premium resources.

Can I play offline?

Yes, the single-player campaign is available offline, but online PvP requires an internet connection.

How do I upgrade my ships?

Earn resources through battles, missions, and events to unlock ship upgrades and new technologies.

What makes Star Force unique?

The game blends real-time strategy with immersive space combat, offering both solo missions and competitive multiplayer.

Are there special events?

Yes! Participate in limited-time galactic events, raid alien strongholds, and earn rare rewards.

Who is the developer?

Star Force is developed by Fusion Star Force Studios, known for creating visually stunning and highly strategic space games.
