How to Play

01

Build Your Fleet

Unlock and customize a variety of starships, each with unique strengths and abilities.

02

Upgrade Your Arsenal

Enhance your weapons, shields, and thrusters to gain a tactical edge.

03

Engage in Battles

Command your fleet in real-time space battles, using strategy to outmaneuver opponents.

04

Conquer New Worlds

Expand your territory, battle against alien forces, and uncover hidden resources.

05

Join the Galactic War

Participate in large-scale conflicts where alliances shape the fate of the universe.

06

Compete in PvP Mode

Challenge other players in ranked battles to prove your dominance.
